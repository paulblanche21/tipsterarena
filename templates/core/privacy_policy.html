Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 06, 2025

At Tipster Arena, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website, services, and applications (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Your name, email address, and username when you register for an account.
  • Profile information, including your avatar, bio, and location, if you choose to provide it.
  • Content you post, such as tips, comments, and messages.
  • Payment information, if applicable, for premium features (processed securely through third-party payment processors).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use the Service, including:

  • Device information, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage data, such as the pages you visit, the time and date of your visits, and the links you click.
  • Location data, if you enable location services, to provide location-based features.

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service, including personalizing your experience.
  • To process your account registration and manage your account.
  • To enable user interactions, such as posting tips, commenting, and messaging.
  • To communicate with you, including sending service-related notifications and responding to your inquiries.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve our Service’s functionality and performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the rights, safety, and security of Tipster Arena, our users, and the public.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your User Content (e.g., tips, comments) and profile information (e.g., username, avatar) are visible to other users as part of the Service’s functionality.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5.1 Account Information

You may update or delete your account information at any time by accessing your profile settings. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us at support@tipsterarena.com.
