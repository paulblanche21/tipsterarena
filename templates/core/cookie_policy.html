Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 06, 2025

Tipster Arena uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website, services, and applications (collectively, the "Service"). This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, analyze site usage, and deliver personalized content. We also use similar technologies, such as local storage and tracking pixels, for these purposes.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Service to function, such as maintaining your login session and enabling core features.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect data on how users interact with the Service to improve its performance and usability.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., language settings) to provide a tailored experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver relevant ads and measure their effectiveness, often set by third-party partners.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties, such as analytics providers or advertisers, to support the Service. We do not control these cookies but work with partners who adhere to applicable data protection standards. Examples include Google Analytics and social media plugins.

  • Cookie Consent Tool: On your first visit, you can customize your cookie preferences via our consent popup.
  • Opt-Out Links: For third-party advertising cookies, visit opt-out pages like NAI Opt-Out or Your Online Choices.

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
