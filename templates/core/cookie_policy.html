Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 06, 2025

Tipster Arena uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content on our website, services, and applications (collectively, the "Service"). This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions. We also use similar technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage, to collect and store information.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. They enable core functionality, such as:

  • Maintaining your login session.
  • Ensuring the security of your account.
  • Allowing you to navigate the Service and use its features.

Essential cookies cannot be disabled, as they are critical to the operation of the Service.

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use the Service, such as which pages you visit and any errors you encounter. We use this data to:

  • Analyze usage patterns and improve the Service’s performance.
  • Monitor the effectiveness of our features and content.
  • Identify and fix technical issues.

Performance cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and are used in an aggregated form.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the Service to remember your preferences and provide enhanced functionality, such as:

  • Remembering your language or region settings.
  • Customizing the appearance of the Service based on your preferences.
  • Storing your choices, such as cookie consent preferences.

2.4 Analytics and Advertising Cookies

We may use third-party analytics and advertising cookies to:

  • Understand how users interact with the Service through tools like Google Analytics.
  • Deliver personalized advertisements based on your interests.
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

These cookies may track your browsing activity across other websites and are subject to the privacy policies of the third-party providers.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Authenticate users and maintain session security.
  • Analyze usage trends to improve the Service’s functionality and user experience.
  • Personalize your experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Deliver relevant content and advertisements.
  • Monitor the performance of the Service and identify technical issues.

4. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or by adjusting your preferences on the Service (if available). Options include:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. You can also set your browser to notify you when cookies are being set. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.
  • Opt-Out Tools: For advertising cookies, you can opt out using tools like the Digital Advertising Alliance’s AdChoices or the Network Advertising Initiative’s opt-out page.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers support "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. We currently do not respond to DNT signals, but you can manage cookies through your browser settings.

For more information on managing cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers, such as analytics and advertising partners, to set cookies on the Service. These cookies are subject to the third parties’ privacy policies. Examples include:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to analyze usage data. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
  • Advertising Networks: We may use third-party advertising networks to deliver personalized ads. You can manage these through the opt-out tools mentioned above.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on the Service with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Cookie Policy.
